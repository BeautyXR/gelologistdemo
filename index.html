Questions

Frequently Asked

What is BIAB and why is it better for natural nails? +
BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a flexible builder gel applied directly to the natural nail. Unlike hard gels or acrylics, it flexes with your natural nail, dramatically reducing breakage. It also requires minimal filing and no aggressive drilling — making it the healthiest option for growing strong, beautiful natural nails.
Do you do nail extensions or acrylics? +
No — I specialise exclusively in natural nails with BIAB overlay. This is a deliberate choice because I believe in protecting and growing your natural nails rather than covering them up. The results speak for themselves.
How long does a BIAB set last? +
A properly applied BIAB set lasts 3–4 weeks with minimal chipping or lifting. I recommend infill appointments every 3 weeks to keep your nails looking their best and to check on your natural nail growth progress.
Are your products vegan and cruelty-free? +
Yes, always. Every product I use is vegan and cruelty-free. If you have specific allergies or sensitivities, please let me know when booking and I'll ensure everything is suitable for you.
Where are you located? +
I'm based in Manchester's Northern Quarter at 3rd Floor, 82 King St, Manchester M2 4WQ. It's a beautiful, calm studio space — the perfect environment for a relaxing nail appointment.
How do I book? +
Book directly through this website or DM me on Instagram @the_gelologist. I recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance — especially for hand-painted and seasonal sets which have very limited availability.
